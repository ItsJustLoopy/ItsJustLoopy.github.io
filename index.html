Godot C#

'Into The Rabbit Hole' is a 2D puzzle rogue-like game made in Godot with custom shaders and tiling system. In the game, the player is a rabbit constantly chased by wild animals and must move quickly, avoiding traps and collecting food while searching for the exit or "the rabbit hole" to escape to the next level. As the player progresses, they can edit the traits of all objects in the game, including the rabbit's appearance, movement, and behavior. The game is designed to be challenging and fun, with a variety of levels and different object types to explore with each playthrough being completely unique.

Monogame C#

'Defcon Null' (project name: Etetgame) is an online multiplayer Hardcore Turn-Based Tactics Game that features 24/7 Hosted Multiplayer Servers, Map editor, and Modding support developed fully within Monogame. The game is a 'squad-based' tactics game where players control a team of units in a variety of game modes.
